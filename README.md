# Amazon_Sales_Dashbord_Analysis
**Amazon Sales Overview:** Revenue ₹83.29M from 128.94K orders, avg order value ₹645. T-shirts lead sales, M/L/XL sizes dominate. Maharashtra tops states, and most orders are fulfilled by Amazon.
The Amazon Sales Dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance using key metrics and visuals. It shows total revenue of ₹83.29M, 128.94K total orders, an average order value of ₹645, and 117K units sold, helping track overall growth.

The monthly revenue trend highlights fluctuations across the year, useful for identifying seasonality. The order status chart shows that most orders are successfully shipped, while some are cancelled, returned, or pending.

In the Top States by Sales, regions like Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Telangana contribute the most revenue. Product analysis reveals that T-shirts and shirts generate the highest sales, followed by blazers and trousers.

The size distribution indicates that M, L, and XL are the most popular sizes. Lastly, the fulfilment section shows Amazon handles most deliveries compared to merchants. Overall, the dashboard helps in understanding sales patterns, customer preferences, and operational efficiency.
